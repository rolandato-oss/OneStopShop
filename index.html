Application Overview

Welcome! This consolidated form is for obtaining the necessary permits for aquaculture operations in Ghana. You will be guided through a step-by-step process.

Important: The Environmental Protection Authority (EPA) and Water Resources Commission (WRC) permits are mandatory prerequisites for most Fisheries Commission (FC) permits.

WRC Exemption: If your primary water source is tap water from Ghana Water Company Limited (GWCL) or Community Water and Sanitation Agency (CWSA), and you pay utility bills, you do not need a WRC permit.
